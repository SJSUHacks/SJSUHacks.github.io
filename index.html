FAQ

When and where will SiliconX Hacks Hackathon take place?
The inaugural SJSU Hacks Hackathon will take place on Saturday April 27th-28th, at the Boccardo Business Center .
What should I bring to the hackathon?
Bring your creativity and gear! Laptops, chargers, notebooks—whatever you need for coding, designing, or presenting. Oh, and don't forget your ID for check-in! 🎨💻🔌 .
Do I need to come with a team?
No team? No problem! We'll pair you up with someone whose skills complement yours! 😎✨ .
Will there be prizes?
Get ready for awesome prizes in our three main tracks (AI, Consumer, and Generalist)! We'll also have extra certificates for categories like Best Overall Hack, Most Creative Idea, Best Design, and more. Stay tuned! 🏆🚀 .
How do I register for SiliconX Hacks?
To sign up, just fill out an application and wait for the green light to join the hackathon! Keep an eye on your inbox for updates on your application status .
Is there a cost to participate?
Nope .
Who can I contact for more information?
Got questions? Shoot us an email at ideas.sjsu@gmail.com or follow us on socials for updates! We're here to make sure you have a blast at SJSU Hacks Hackathon! 🚀✨ .
Who can participate in SiliconX Hacks?
SiliconX Hacks welcomes all SJSU students, clubs, and pals! Whether you're a coding ninja, a startup dreamer, a design fanatic, or just keen to learn and make cool stuff, this event is your jam. All backgrounds and skill levels, come on down! 🎉✨
