SJSU

FILLER San Jose State University (SJSU) is a renowned institution located in the heart of Silicon Valley, California. Established in 1857, it's the oldest public university on the West Coast. With a diverse student body and over 145 areas of study across eight colleges, SJSU offers academic excellence and opportunities for success. Its vibrant campus, rich with both historic and modern architecture, provides an inspiring environment for learning and collaboration. Beyond academics, SJSU's location offers unmatched access to internships and industry connections, while its diverse student organizations and inclusive community foster personal growth and development. With a faculty committed to mentorship and innovation, SJSU prepares students to excel in their fields and make meaningful contributions to society.
